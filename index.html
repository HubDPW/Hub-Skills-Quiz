Professional Skills Diagnostic

Answer the 25 questions below. When you submit, the diagnostic scores all 25 capability areas and recommends courses from the combined skills and interpersonal skills catalogue.

Scale: 1 = Very Low, 2 = Low, 3 = Moderate, 4 = Strong, 5 = Excellent

1. Accountability

How consistently do you take ownership for your commitments, decisions, and follow-through at work?

2. Assertiveness and Self-Confidence

How confident are you in expressing your views, needs, and boundaries clearly in professional situations?

3. Difficult Conversations and Conflict

How effectively do you manage tension, disagreement, or uncomfortable conversations with others?

4. Active Listening and Clarity

How well do you listen carefully, ask useful questions, and make sure others feel understood?

5. Communication Clarity

How effectively do you communicate ideas clearly through speaking, writing, or everyday interactions?

6. Presentation Impact

How confident are you in presenting ideas in a way that engages and influences an audience?

7. Meeting Effectiveness

How effectively do you contribute to meetings, brainstorming sessions, and group discussions?

8. Customer Orientation

How effectively do you understand, anticipate, and respond to customer or stakeholder needs?

9. Handling Objections and Dissatisfaction

How comfortable are you managing complaints, objections, or dissatisfied customers constructively?

10. Curiosity and Learning Agility

How actively do you ask questions, explore ideas, and learn from unfamiliar situations or people?

11. Emotional Intelligence

How well do you understand and manage your own emotions and respond appropriately to the emotions of others?

12. Negotiation

How confident are you in negotiating outcomes that protect relationships while creating value for all sides?

13. Networking

How actively do you build and maintain useful professional relationships and networks?

14. Cross-Cultural Collaboration

How effectively do you work with colleagues or stakeholders from different cultures, backgrounds, and working styles?

15. Persuasion and Influence

How effectively do you influence others to support your ideas, recommendations, or decisions?

16. Teamwork and Collaboration

How effectively do you collaborate with others to achieve shared outcomes, especially in distributed or hybrid work?

17. Critical Thinking

How confident are you in analyzing information, challenging assumptions, and identifying weak reasoning?

18. Creativity and Design Thinking

How comfortable are you generating new ideas, framing problems creatively, and testing practical solutions?

19. Decision-Making

How effectively do you evaluate options and make sound, timely decisions with others when needed?

20. Complex Problem Solving

How well do you handle problems with multiple variables, uncertainty, or competing priorities?

21. Productivity and Time Management

How effectively do you manage your time, focus, workload, and competing demands?

22. Remote and Hybrid Working

How effectively do you work, collaborate, and stay organized in remote or hybrid environments?

23. Career Growth and New Roles

How proactively do you build your career direction, develop new capabilities, and adapt to role transitions?

24. Self-Knowledge and Reflection

How well do you understand your strengths, motivators, growth areas, and patterns of behavior?

25. Wellbeing and Resilience

How well do you maintain personal wellbeing, mental balance, and resilience under work pressure?
